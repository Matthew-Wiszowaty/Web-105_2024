Rainbow Six Siege

Rainbow Six Siege is an online multi-player shooter game which involves teamwork and strategy. The game has a very large active community and 70 different characters to choose from.

Gameplaying

In Rainbow Six Siege there are two sides, defenders and attackers. Each side takes turns swapping until somebody wins best of 9. The defenders defend two bomb sites on the map that are in adjacent rooms and attackers try their best to defuse it while eliminating as many of the defenders as possible.
