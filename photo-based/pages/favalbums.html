My Discovery of Music

My love for music began through video game and movie soundtracks. As I played games and watched films, I found myself captivated by the way music enhanced the experience, stirring emotions and setting the tone for each scene. Whether it was the epic orchestral scores of fantasy games transporting me to other worlds or the haunting melodies of cinematic soundtracks evoking powerful emotions, I realized the profound impact music could have. These soundtracks sparked my curiosity and led me to explore different genres and artists, ultimately shaping my diverse musical taste. From there, my passion for music only grew, as I discovered the endless possibilities of expression through sound.
