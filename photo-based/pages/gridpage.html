Peripherals

"Peripheral" typically refers to devices that are external to a computer or other electronic device, but are connected to it in some way to provide additional functionality or input/output capabilities. Common examples of peripherals include keyboards, mice, monitors, printers, scanners, external hard drives, speakers, and webcams. These devices extend the capabilities of the main computing device and are essential for user interaction and data transfer.
