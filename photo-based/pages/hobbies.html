Cycling

My Cycling Hobby

Ever since I was a kid, I have loved to go cycling. I use cycling as my main method of cardio and I also use it to explore new places and find new routes. My current bike is a Trek FX 1 and I swapped the standard flat bars for drop bars found on many higher end road bikes. I have put about 300 miles on it since I swapped the bars and I love it. I am also saving up for a nicer bike similar to the Specialized SL6 Tarmac or in a similar budget. I unfortunately do not see my current bike surviving for much longer as after one summer of 300 moderately hard miles the chain has drastically stretched and the rear derailleur gear bearings are starting to seize. However, those parts are not incredibly difficult to replace.
